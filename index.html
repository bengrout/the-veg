The Veg is a collection of recipes that we have cooked for the Brighton Choir With No Name.

All of our recipes are vegan or can be easily adapted, and have been used to feed our choir of 45+ members and volunteers.

Crispy noodle and aubergine

Crispy stir-fried noodles with aubergines and a spicy hoisin sauce.

Chilli con mushroom

Vegan chilli with a sweet and smokey BBQ flavour

Cauliflower and paneer curry

Mild but flavoursome paneer (or tofu) curry

Green minestrone with tortellini

Green vegetable soup with pasta. Give peas a chance.

Spicy chickpea curry

Tomato based curry with plenty of kick

Tofu teriyaki

Crispy tofu in a terikiyaki sauce

If you would like to contribute a recipe please email hello@theveg.org
