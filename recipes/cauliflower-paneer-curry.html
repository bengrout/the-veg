Cauliflower and paneer (or tofu) curry

Ingredients for 4 servings

  • 2 Aubergines
  • 2 Red peppers
  • 1 Small cauliflower
  • 2 Onions
  • 2cm Root ginger
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1tsp Mustard seeds
  • 1tsp Turmeric
  • 1tsp Cumin seeds
  • 0.5tsp Chilli flakes
  • 400g Can chopped tomatoes
  • 250ml Vegetable stock
  • 175g Paneer or tofu
  • Handful of fresh coriander chopped

Method

  1. Break cauliflower into florets, chop the pepper into chunks, and cube the aubergine
  2. Put the veg into the oven at 200c with a drizzle of oil and pinch of salt - bake for 20 minutes (turn once)
  3. Chop half the onions into rings - fry in a small amount of oil until crispy and brown
  4. Dice the remaining onions, mince the ginger and garlic. Prepare the stock.
  5. Add the diced onions to a large pot and fry for 5 minutes, then add the spices, ginger and garlic and fry for 2 minutes.
  6. Add the vegetables from the oven and stir in to cover with spices.
  7. Add the chopped tomatoes and stock and bring to the boil and simmer for 20 minutes. Cube the paneer and add for the last 5 minutes. Season with salt, pepper and fresh coriander.
  8. Serve with rice, Greek yoghurt and naan.
