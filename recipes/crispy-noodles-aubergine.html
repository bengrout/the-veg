Crispy noodles and aubergine

Ingredients for 2 servings

  • 1 Aubergine
  • 1 Pepper
  • 2 shallots or spring onions
  • 2tsp ginger
  • 1tsp Sriracha
  • 4tsp Hoisin
  • 1 Noodle nest
  • 1tbsp Sesame oil

Method

  1. Prepare by halving the aubergine and slicing into finger sized pieces; dice the peppers and onions; and mince the ginger.
  2. Boil the noodle nest until soft and then drain well and leave to dry for a couple of minutes
  3. In a large pan or wok, heat the sesame oil and add the noodles to one half of the pan. In the other half of the pan, add the onion followed by the ginger
  4. A minute later, add the aubergine and peppers
  5. Stir-fry the vegetables for five minutes while trying not to disturb the noodles
  6. Mix the hoisin and Sriracha in a small jug and then add to the pan stirring into the vegatables
  7. Serve in a bowl. Add a sprinkling of spring onion if you have any spare
