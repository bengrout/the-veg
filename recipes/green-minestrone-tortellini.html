Green minestrone and tortellini

Ingredients for 8 servings

  • 3tbsp garlic flavoured oil
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 450g frozen peas
  • 2 leeks (Halved lengthwise and thinly sliced, or cheat and use 300g frozen leeks)
  • 1 large potato (peeled and finely diced)
  • 1 celery stalk (finely diced)
  • 2 courgettes (finely diced)
  • 225g green beans (trimmed and halved, or cheat and go frozen)
  • 250ml cold water
  • 2tsp salt
  • 2 cans of cannellini beans aka white kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 450g spinach and ricotta tortellini (1.5 supermarket packets), or regular pasta as a vegan alternative
  • A handful of basil leaves
  • 2tbsp grated parmesan, vegeterian hard cheese, or vegan alternative

Method

  1. Prepare the Leeks, Potato, Celery, Courgette and Green Beans
  2. Warm the oil in a large pan and stir in the thyme
  3. Add the peas and stir in for a minute
  4. Add the Leeks, Potato, Celery, Courgette and Green Beans and stir
  5. Add the water and salt. Put the lid on the pan and bring to the boil.
  6. Remove the lid and simmer for at least 15 minutes until the potatoes are tender
  7. Remove 3 ladles of mixture (solids, not liquid). Put it in a large bowl.
  8. Add basil and parmesan to the bowl and blend into a green puree with a stick blender or food processor.
  9. Add the cannellini beans to the pan and leave for 5 minutes
  10. Add the tortellini to the pan, bring to the boil and turn off the heat
  11. Serve in bowls with fresh bread
