Tofu teriyaki

Ingredients for 6 servings

Teriyaki sauce

  • 120ml water
  • 10tbsp light soy sauce (about 170ml)
  • 7tbsp brown sugar (about 85g)
  • 4tbsp rice vinegar (about 80ml)
  • 4tbsp mirin (about 80ml)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1tbsp minced ginger
  • 3 tbsp corn starch (about 25g)
  • Sesame oil

Tofu

  • 600g pack of firm tofu (Tofuking is good value - you can find it in many Asian supermarkets)
  • 2tbsp corn starch
  • 2tbsp light soy suace
  • 1tbsp sesame oil

Method

Teriyaki sauce

  1. Heat a tbsp of sesame oil (or other nutty oil) in a saucepan on a medium heat
  2. Add the ginger and garlic shortly followed by the water, soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, and mirin (basically everything apart from the cornstarch)
  3. Leave on a low heat for at least 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. To thicken the sauce, mix 75ml cold water with 3tbsp of cornstarch and add to the pan
  5. Stir in well and remove from the heat

Crispy tofu

  1. Press liquid out of tofu using kitchen roll
  2. Cut the tofu into cubes (inch cubes)
  3. Toss in bowl with soy sauce and cornstarch
  4. Bake in oven for 30mins at 200c (turn halfway)

You can serve the tofu with rice and pour over the sauce. You can also stir fry some green beans, brocolli, and spring onions in garlic and ginger to go with it.
