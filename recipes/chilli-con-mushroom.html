Chilli con mushroom

Ingredients for 5 servings

  • 2 red or green peppers (sliced)
  • 1 Can of tinned tomatoes
  • 1 Can of kidney beans (drained)
  • 200g mushrooms (very finely diced)
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1/4 jar jalape√±os (optional)
  • 1tbsp cajun mix
  • 1/2tbsp cumin
  • 1tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  • 4tbsp BBQ sauce
  • Serve with jacket potatoes or rice

Method

  1. Warm a large sauce pan on a medium heat and soften the onions with a splash of oil and then add the garlic
  2. After a few minutes, add the cajun mix, chilli flakes, and cumin and stir in well. Don't let the spices burn in the pan.
  3. Add the peppers and stir in with the spicy onions and cook through for a couple of minutes
  4. Empty the can of tomatoes into the pan, and then fill the can a quarter way with cold water and add to the pot. Stir well.
  5. Bring to the boil and then simmer for 5 minutes before adding the mushrooms and drained kidney beans. The finer you can dice the mushrooms the better - when finely diced they are a great substitute for minced beef.
  6. Keep simmering for another ten minutes before adding the jalapenos and BBQ sauce. Stir ocassionally and leave to simmer for at least another 10 minutes. The longer you leave it the tastier it gets.
  7. Serve over jacket potatoes or rice
